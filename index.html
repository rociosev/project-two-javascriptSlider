A Quick Guide to Field Hockey

Getting started in Field Hockey!

Use the arrows to navigate through this tutorial.
Hover over the to see the images' description.
Place your right hand on the bottom of the grip, and the left one on the top.
Put your heels parallel to each other, stand with your knees flexed and your back bent. Place the ball a few inches in front of your feet, and between them.
Always aim to have the ball and your stick together to have better control.
When you run you should do so with the ball and stick besides your body, not in front, or behind.
