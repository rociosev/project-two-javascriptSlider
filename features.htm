This are the stick and ball that are used, the stick is rounded on one side of the bottom and flat on the other. Use the flat side to hit the ball.
How you grab the stick is very important, you must put your right hand in the bottom and the left one one the top.
Your left hand is the one that controls the stick, the right one simply supports it.
You must stand with your knees slightly flexed so that your stick is diagonally across your body and it touches the ground.
The goal of Field Hockey is that the ball does not come off your stick, aim to always have the ball and your stick having this relationship.
To drible you must flip your stick over the ball to make sure you only touch the ball with the flat side.
